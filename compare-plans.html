Frequently Asked Questions

Which tier is right for me?

It depends on your current revenue and growth goals. Growth is perfect for businesses under $20K/month, Accelerator for $20-50K/month, and Empire for $50K+ looking to dominate their market.

Can I upgrade tiers later?

Absolutely! Many clients start with Growth and upgrade as they scale. We'll credit 50% of your Growth setup fee toward Accelerator, or 100% toward Empire.

What's included in the setup fee?

The setup fee covers all AI development, customization, training, and implementation. This includes building your AI agents, training GPTs, customizing scripts, and onboarding your team.

How long until I see results?

Most clients see efficiency improvements within 7 days. Revenue increases typically start within 30 days, with full ROI achieved within 90 days for most tiers.

What if I'm not tech-savvy?

No problem! We handle all technical setup and provide simple, user-friendly interfaces. If you can use email and social media, you can use our AI systems.

Is there a contract commitment?

Growth and Accelerator require 6-month commitments, Empire requires 12 months. After that, it's month-to-month. We're confident you'll want to stay once you see the results.
