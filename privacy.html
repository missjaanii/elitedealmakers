Privacy Policy

At Elite Dealmakers, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our digital product creation services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, business name
  • Business Information: Your expertise area, target audience, business goals
  • Payment Information: Billing details (processed securely through payment partners)
  • Content Information: Materials and information you provide for product creation

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and deliver our digital product creation services
  • Communicate with you about your project
  • Process payments and maintain records
  • Send updates about your order status
  • Improve our services based on feedback
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

We DO NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in these circumstances:

  • With service providers who assist in our operations (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • To comply with legal obligations or court orders
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • With your explicit consent

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Secure encrypted connections for data transmission
  • Limited access to personal information (need-to-know basis)
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Secure cloud storage for project files

5. Your Content & Intellectual Property

Any content you provide for product creation remains your property. We use it solely to create your digital products and do not claim any ownership rights. Upon project completion and full payment, all created materials become your exclusive property.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for:

  • Project files: 6 months after delivery (for revision purposes)
  • Communication records: 2 years for service history
  • Financial records: As required by tax laws (typically 7 years)

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information we hold
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Receive a copy of your data in a portable format

8. Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies to enhance user experience. We use analytics to understand website usage and improve our services. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. Please review their privacy policies separately.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes via email or through our website.

12. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or concerns, contact us at:

  • Email: support@elitedealmakers.in
  • Response Time: Within 48 hours

13. Consent

By using our services, you consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
